Te Whare Tapa Whā Wellbeing Plan

This wellbeing plan is based on Tā Mason Durie’s model of Te Whare Tapa Whā. It supports my holistic wellbeing while studying at Dev Academy.

Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing)

  • Go for a walk before class once a week

Taha Whānau (Family Wellbeing)

  • Have dinner with family every evening
  • Call a friend or loved one once a week

Taha Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing)

  • Listen to a guided meditation before sleep
  • Journal my thoughts once a week

Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing)

  • Take 5 minutes daily to reflect and express gratitude

Whenua (Connection to the Land)

  • Walk barefoot on grass once a week
  • Do a nature walk every Sunday
