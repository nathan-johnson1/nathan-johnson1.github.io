Learning Plan

1. What is your long-term goal or career pathway?

My long-term goal is to hopefully build a career in software development and hopefully succeed in sticking to this pathway and eventually becoming an experienced programmer.

2. A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

I think my strengths when it comes to learning are that I am able to stay focused on a task and responsibly manage my time. My limitations are that I sometimes can do a limited amount of research or just the bare minimum required to complete a task and I don’t always absorb all of the information I need.

3. What skills (non-technical/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I would specifically like to see myself improve in teamwork aspects and effectively communicating well with my peers. I also need to learn to ask for help more and know when I’m stuck and need help.

4. A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme

How I will work productively and safely with others

I will manage myself by communicating clearly with peers and facilitators, meeting deadlines, and staying organized. I’ll contribute to group projects respectfully, follow coding best practices, and ensure a safe and inclusive environment, both online and in person.

What I will do when things are building up and starting to get to me

The best way for me to relax myself when things are getting to me is to just take a small break, by either going for a small drive, or a walk to the kitchen for a cup of coffee.

5. A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way

My first course of action for seeking help is to first head to google and see if anyone else has had the same issues as me, or I will go to the discord help desk channel and ask for help.

6. A description of what you expect from the Facilitation team

From the facilitation team, I would only expect timely replies when I ask for help, or constructive feedback to my work, but I understand I have to take initiative for my own learning and control my own workflow accordingly.

7. Any scheduling information such as block-out times

I do not have any serious commitments at the moment, but I may have one-off events where I am unavailable to study.

Nathan Johnson 2025.
