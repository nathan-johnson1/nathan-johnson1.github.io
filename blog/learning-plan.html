Bootcamp Learning Plan

1. What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

My long-term goal is to become a confident, full-time software developer working in a team where I can keep growing and building real-world projects.

2. What are you hoping to use the skills you gain during this course for? What impact would you like to make once you leave Dev Academy? Do you have a destination in mind?

I want to use these skills to contribute meaningfully to a dev team, ideally in a junior developer role. I’d love to eventually work on tools or software that help people solve real problems.

3. What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?

I’m good at managing my time and staying focused. But I can struggle with asking for help or reaching out when stuck, which I’ll need to improve during Bootcamp.

4. Re-evaluate your strengths and limitations from Sprint 2. Has your idea of these changed throughout Foundations? Have you discovered any new ones during this course? How do you think these will help/hinder you during Bootcamp?

My strengths still include focus and independence, but Foundations helped me see how important collaboration is. I’ve also noticed I can overthink things when working alone, so checking in with others will help keep me grounded during Bootcamp.

5. What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

Probably staying balanced with the intensity of the schedule. Also adjusting to more regular teamwork after mostly working solo.

6. What non-technical skills - human skills - would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I’d like to get better at asking for help, pairing with others, and giving/receiving feedback without overthinking it.

7. What are your expectations from the Bootcamp team?

Mainly clear guidance when needed and timely support if I raise something. I also value honest feedback.

8. What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp?

To stay engaged, communicate clearly, and be proactive, especially in group situations or when stuck.

9. Based on your knowledge of Bootcamp, how and where will you seek help in a timely way?

I’ll use Discord, talk to peers, and reach out to facilitators. I’ll aim not to sit stuck for too long before asking.

10. Write down a commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme.

I’ll manage my workload by planning each day, staying organised, and communicating with my team. I’ll take short breaks to avoid burnout and check in regularly with others to stay on track.
